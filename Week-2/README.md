# 👨‍💼 Employee Attrition Prediction

## 📌 Project Overview

Employee attrition is a major challenge for organizations because losing experienced employees increases hiring costs, training efforts and impacts overall productivity.

The objective of this project is to analyze employee data, identify the factors associated with employee attrition and provide practical recommendations that can help the HR team improve employee retention.

---

# 🎯 Objectives

- Understand employee attrition patterns.
- Identify employees who are more likely to leave.
- Compare multiple machine learning models.
- Generate business insights for HR decision-making.

---

# 📂 Dataset

The project uses the IBM HR Employee Attrition dataset containing employee information such as:

- Age
- Department
- Job Role
- Monthly Income
- Overtime
- Job Satisfaction
- Work-Life Balance
- Years at Company
- Distance From Home
- Education
- Performance Rating

---

# ⚙️ Project Workflow

### 1️⃣ Data Loading & Exploration

- Loaded the dataset
- Explored dataset structure
- Identified target variable
- Checked missing values

---

### 2️⃣ Data Cleaning & Preprocessing

- Removed unnecessary columns
- Encoded categorical variables
- Applied feature scaling
- Built preprocessing pipeline using ColumnTransformer

---

### 3️⃣ Exploratory Data Analysis

The following analyses were performed:

- Attrition by Department
- Attrition by Job Role
- Attrition vs Monthly Income
- Attrition vs Work-Life Balance
- Attrition vs Years at Company

---

### 4️⃣ Model Building

The following classification models were trained:

- Logistic Regression
- Random Forest Classifier
- Gradient Boosting Classifier

---

### 5️⃣ Model Evaluation

The models were evaluated using:

- Precision
- Recall
- F1 Score
- ROC-AUC Score
- Confusion Matrix

---

### 6️⃣ Visualizations

Project visualizations include:

- Department-wise Attrition
- Monthly Income Box Plot
- Confusion Matrix
- Feature Importance
- ROC Curve Comparison

---

### 7️⃣ HR Recommendations

Business recommendations were prepared to help HR improve employee retention based on the findings obtained from the analysis.

---

# 📈 Key Findings

- Sales department showed the highest employee attrition.
- Sales Representatives experienced the highest exit rate.
- Monthly Income, Overtime and Age were among the most influential factors associated with employee attrition.
- Employee attrition was influenced by multiple workplace factors rather than salary alone.

---

# 💡 Business Recommendations

- Improve employee engagement within high-attrition departments.
- Monitor overtime and workload regularly.
- Review salary growth and career development opportunities.
- Conduct periodic employee feedback sessions.
